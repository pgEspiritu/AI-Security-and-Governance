# 🔎 AI Model Discovery

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the use of **enterprise data with AI** are at the heart of future innovation. However, the rapid adoption of AI also introduces **significant privacy and security risks**.

One of the major concerns is the rise of **Shadow AI**, where AI systems are developed or deployed **without formal oversight or governance**.

**AI Model Discovery** addresses these challenges by identifying both **sanctioned and unsanctioned AI models** across an organization's infrastructure, including:

- ☁️ Public clouds  
- 🏢 Private environments  
- 📦 SaaS applications  

This process provides a **complete understanding of an organization's AI ecosystem**, ensuring full visibility into all AI models, including those operating silently in the background.

---

# 🌐 AI Models Across Different Environments

AI Model Discovery involves identifying and understanding all AI models deployed across an organization, regardless of where they are located.

These models may include:

- In-house developed models
- Vendor-provided models
- Open-source AI models
- Unsanctioned or shadow AI systems

These can exist in several environments.

---

## ☁️ Public Clouds

Organizations must discover all AI models running on cloud platforms such as:

- AWS
- Microsoft Azure
- Google Cloud Platform (GCP)

This includes models running in:

- Production environments
- Development environments
- Testing environments

Identifying these models ensures organizations understand **how cloud-based AI systems are being used**.

---

## 📦 SaaS Applications

Many **Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)** platforms integrate AI capabilities.

Examples include:

- Marketing automation tools using **LLMs**
- Fraud detection systems in **financial applications**
- AI-powered analytics platforms

Organizations must conduct **dedicated assessments** to collect details about these AI models.

---

## 🏢 Private Environments

AI models may also operate in **on-premises infrastructure or private clouds**.

Discovery efforts should identify:

- Custom-built AI models
- Internal LLM deployments
- Machine learning models used for internal operations

These models often support **specific business objectives** and may otherwise go unnoticed.

---

# 👁️ Achieving Full Visibility of AI Systems

By covering **all computing environments**, AI Model Discovery creates a **holistic view of the AI landscape**.

This process helps organizations:

- Identify sanctioned AI models
- Detect shadow AI systems
- Understand how AI is used across departments
- Improve governance and security

The first step is to **identify all AI models currently operating in the organization**.

---

# 📋 Documenting AI Model Information

For every discovered AI model, organizations should document essential information.

## 🎯 Model Purpose

Describe the intended use of the AI model.

Examples include:

- Customer behavior prediction
- Fraud detection
- Recommendation systems
- Customer support automation

---

## 📊 Training Data

Document the **data used to train the model**, including:

- Data sources
- Data volume
- Data types
- Whether personal or sensitive data is included

---

## 🧠 Model Architecture

Provide a description of the model's architecture.

This may include:

- Neural network design
- Algorithms used
- Special architectural features

---

## 🔄 Inputs and Outputs

Record the model's **input requirements and output results**, including:

- Input data formats
- Output types
- Expected value ranges
- Processing pipelines

---

## 🔗 Integration Points

Identify how the AI model interacts with other systems.

This includes:

- APIs
- Data pipelines
- External integrations
- Manual processes

Understanding these integration points helps organizations **map AI dependencies across systems**.

---

# 🪪 Model Cards

**Model Cards** are concise documents that accompany AI models.

They summarize key information such as:

- Intended use
- Model performance
- Limitations
- Ethical considerations

Model cards are valuable for multiple stakeholders.

### 👨‍💻 AI Practitioners
Gain insights into **model capabilities and applications**.

### 🧑‍💻 Developers
Compare models and guide **future improvements and development**.

### 🏛️ Policymakers
Assess the **social, legal, and policy implications** of AI systems.

### 🏢 Organizations
Use model cards to support **AI tool adoption decisions**.

### 🔐 Privacy Professionals
Evaluate how **personal data is used** and assess potential impacts on individuals.

---

# 📚 Cataloging AI Models for Full Visibility

After identifying AI models, organizations must organize them into a **centralized catalog**.

---

## 🕵️ Shadow AI Detection

Organizations must actively search for **shadow AI systems**.

Shadow AI refers to:

> AI models or tools developed or deployed **without formal approval or oversight**.

These systems are often created by individual teams to address **localized business needs**.

Detecting shadow AI is critical to ensure:

- Security
- Compliance
- Ethical AI usage

---

## 📖 Catalog Creation

All documented information should be compiled into a **central AI model catalog**.

This catalog should:

- Be easily accessible
- Provide clear documentation
- Serve as the **single source of truth** for AI systems

---

## 🔁 Review and Validation

The AI model catalog must be **regularly reviewed and updated**.

This process includes:

- Adding newly discovered models
- Updating model details
- Removing obsolete systems

Maintaining an accurate catalog ensures **continuous governance and transparency**.

---

# 📈 Benefits of AI Model Discovery

Completing the AI discovery process helps organizations:

- 🔍 Gain full visibility into AI systems  
- 🛡️ Strengthen governance and security  
- 📊 Improve risk management  
- ⚙️ Optimize AI resource usage  
- 🔄 Avoid duplication of AI solutions  
- 🤝 Enable better collaboration across teams  

Ultimately, AI Model Discovery helps organizations **use AI more effectively and responsibly**.

# 🔮 Trends in AI Model Discovery

AI model discovery is evolving as organizations adopt more sophisticated technologies.

---

## 🤖 Automated Discovery and Cataloging

Advances in **machine learning and automation** are enabling automated AI discovery tools.

These tools can:

- Scan infrastructure environments
- Detect AI models automatically
- Update AI inventories in real time

This reduces **manual effort** and improves accuracy.

---

## 📏 Standardization and Interoperability

There is growing interest in developing **standardized formats and protocols** for representing AI model information.

Standardization would enable:

- Easier integration with IT systems
- Better collaboration between teams
- Improved interoperability across tools

---

## 🌍 Cross-Domain Collaboration

AI is increasingly used across multiple industries.

Future AI model discovery systems will enable:

- Cross-industry knowledge sharing
- Integration of models across domains
- Collaborative innovation

These systems must also ensure:

- Privacy protection
- Intellectual property safeguards
